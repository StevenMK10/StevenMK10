About Us

We are a passionate group committed to conserving the natural beauty of our environment. Our platform allows you to reserve eco-friendly spots, ensuring sustainable tourism and minimal environmental impact.

FAQs

  • How does environment reservation work?
    You can select and reserve eco-spots for various activities, ensuring the location is preserved for future use.
  • What kind of places can I reserve?
    Our platform features parks, beaches, forests, and other protected nature reserves.
  • Is there a fee to reserve?
    Most reservations are free, but certain spots may require a minimal fee for maintenance.
